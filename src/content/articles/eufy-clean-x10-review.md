## Who it's for

If you live with a shedding pet and you're tired of unpicking hair from a
tangled brush roller, the eufy Clean X10 Pro Omni is built for you. Everything
about it — the brush design, the suction, the self-cleaning dock — is aimed at
keeping the robot working without constant intervention. That focus is exactly
why it's our pet-hair pick.

## Cleaning performance

On carpet and hard floors alike, the X10 Pro Omni cleans confidently. Suction is
strong enough to lift embedded debris from low- and medium-pile carpet, and it
sensibly ramps power up when it detects carpet and eases off on hard floors.

The standout is how it handles hair. The **anti-tangle dual brushes** genuinely
resist wrapping, which is the failure point that quietly ruins the experience on
lesser robots. Over our testing it kept going where a single-roller design would
have needed rescuing.

## Navigation and obstacles

LiDAR mapping is quick and accurate, and the robot cleans in orderly rows rather
than the random bouncing of cheaper models. It handles typical household
obstacles — cables, shoes, bowls — with sensible avoidance, and low light makes
no difference to how well it navigates.

## The docking station

This is where the "Omni" name earns out. The dock **empties the robot** into a
larger bag and **washes the mop pads**, so upkeep drops to emptying the dust bag
every few weeks and managing the water tanks. It's the feature that turns a
robot vacuum from a gadget into a genuine appliance.

The trade-off is size. Like all full docking stations, it's tall and takes up
real floor space, so decide where it will live before you commit.

## Mopping

Mopping is capable and useful for maintenance passes on sealed hard floors, and
the self-washing pads mean it doesn't smear dirt around. It's a solid bonus — but
it isn't the reason to buy this robot. If class-leading mopping is your priority,
look elsewhere; if pet hair is, you're in the right place.

## The verdict

The eufy Clean X10 Pro Omni does the hard part brilliantly: it keeps cleaning,
reliably, in a home full of pet hair, while asking almost nothing of you thanks
to its self-maintaining dock. Mopping and the dock's footprint are real but minor
compromises. For pet owners especially, it's an easy robot to recommend.
