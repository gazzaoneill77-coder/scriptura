Free browser games, built for the hard road

NARROW. ARCADE is a small collection of original arcade games that run instantly in your browser — no download, no install, no account. Each game is hand-built with plain HTML5 canvas, so it loads fast and plays smoothly on phones, tablets and desktops alike. Tap any cabinet on the wall above to launch it full-screen, or press its number key.

The games

1. RALLY

Endless pong. Return the ball forever against a relentless AI. Keep the ball in play. Every return you make scores a point — miss it once and the round ends. Controls: Pointer / ↑ ↓ — move paddle.

2. SIEGE

Break every brick, then break more. Three lives, rising speed. Bounce the ball off your bat to smash every brick. You have three lives before the siege breaks. Controls: Pointer / ← → — move bat.

3. SERPENT

The classic grid snake. Grow long, never eat yourself. Eat the red squares to grow longer. Hit a wall or your own tail and it is over. Controls: Arrows / WASD — steer.

4. STARFALL

Dodge the falling wall of blocks. Survive as long as you can. Weave your ship through the falling blocks. Your score is how long you survive — do not get hit. Controls: Pointer / ← → — move.

5. ROADRUNNER

An endless runner down the hard road. Jump every obstacle. Jump the obstacles on the road. Distance is your score — a single collision ends the run. Controls: Tap / Space — jump.

6. GATECRASH

A reflex test. Hit each gate ring before it closes. Tap each ring before its timer closes. Let three slip past and you are out. Controls: Tap / click the rings.

How to play

Click or tap a cabinet to open it. Read the objective on the instructions panel, then press PLAY (or tap the screen / press any key) to begin. While playing you can channel-surf to the next game with the CH buttons, reopen the instructions with ? HOW, share a game with a direct link, or press EXIT / ESC to return to the wall. Your best score and play counts are saved on your own device — nothing is uploaded.

Questions

Do I need to download or install anything to play?

No. Every game in NARROW. ARCADE runs instantly in your browser using HTML5 canvas. There is nothing to download, no plugin, and no account required — tap a game and play.

Is NARROW. ARCADE free?

Yes. All six games are free to play, on desktop and mobile, with no ads interrupting play and no paywall.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes. The arcade is mobile-first. Every game supports one-thumb pointer controls, and the games that use a keyboard on desktop fall back to touch on mobile.

Are my high scores saved?

Your best score and play counts are saved locally on your own device using localStorage. They are private to you — the site does not upload them anywhere.
